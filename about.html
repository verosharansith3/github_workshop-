About Elon Musk

Elon Musk, born in Pretoria, South Africa, is one of the world’s most influential entrepreneurs. Known for his relentless drive and futuristic vision, Musk has led groundbreaking innovations in space exploration, electric vehicles, and artificial intelligence.

He is the founder and CEO of SpaceX, co-founder of Tesla, Neuralink, and The Boring Company, and former chairman of SolarCity. His work continuously pushes the boundaries of technology and challenges humanity to dream beyond Earth.
